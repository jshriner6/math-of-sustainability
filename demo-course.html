    Chapter 2 Measuring

    Section 2.1 Units

    Units give measurements meaning. If units are ignored or mis-used, this could lead us to make (at best) inaccurate or (at worst) harmful conclusions. The purpose of this activity will be to practice working with and communicating measurements that contain units.
    The objectives of this activity are that you can...

    Objectives

    1. convert a measurement from one unit to another.
    2. communicate the meaning of a measurement by relating it to human experience.
